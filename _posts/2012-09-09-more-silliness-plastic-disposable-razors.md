<p>We are now aware - or should be aware - that anything that is &quot;plastic and disposable&quot; should be avoided whenever possible. Plastic is a petroleum product and isn&#39;t bio-degradable, as horrifically evidenced by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Pacific_Garbage_Patch">Great Pacific Garbage Patch</a>.</p><p>So why do people use these silly 3, 4 and 5-bladed disposable plastic razors when the humble <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Safety_razor">double-edge safety razor</a> works fine, with it&#39;s disposable metal blades. A good safety razor will last multiple lifetimes (I use my grandfather&#39;s) and - as long as moderate care is taken - will give a close shave with no worries of being cut.</p>
