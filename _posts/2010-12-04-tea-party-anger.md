<p>Message to the Tea Party - What took you so long to get angry?<br />

You didn&#39;t get mad when the Supreme Court stopped a legal recount and appointed a President.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when the Supreme Court decided corporations were people that could secretly fund political elections.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when Cheney allowed Energy company officials to dictate Energy policy and push us to invade Iraq.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when a covert CIA operative got outed.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when the Patriot Act got passed.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when we illegally invaded a country that posed no threat to us. You didn&#39;t get mad when we spent over 800 billion (and counting) on said illegal war.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when the Bush administration borrowed more money from foreign sources than the previous 42 Presidents combined.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when over 10 billion dollars in cash just disappeared in Iraq .<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when you found out we were torturing people.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when the Republican administration embraced trade and outsourcing policies that shipped 6 million American jobs out of the country.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when the government was illegally wiretapping Americans.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when we didn&#39;t catch Bin Laden because we &quot;needed&quot; to invade Iraq.</p><p>You didn&#39;t get mad when the Republican administration rang up 10 trillion dollars in combined budget and current account deficits.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when you saw the horrible conditions at Walter Reed.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when we let a major US city, New Orleans, drown.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when we gave people who had more money than they could spend, the filthy rich, over a trillion dollars in tax breaks.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad with the worst 8 years of job creations in several decades.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when Federal regulations looked the other way while banks and Wall Street reaped billions writing faulty mortgages, short-sold the debt and even wagered that the debts would fail.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when over 200,000 US Citizens lost their lives because they had no health insurance.<br />
<br />
You didn&#39;t get mad when lack of oversight and regulations from the Bush Administration caused US Citizens to lose 12 trillion dollars in investments, retirement, and home values.<br />
<br />
No.....You finally got mad<br />
<br />
When a black man was elected President and decided that all the people in America deserved the right to see a doctor if they were sick.</p><br />
