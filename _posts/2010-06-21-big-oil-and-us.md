<p><em>From a recent email I sent to friends:</em></p><p>I am angered at the pervasive causes of the spill in the Gulf that are far more wide reaching than the terrible effect the oil is having on the environment. &nbsp;Our country&#39;s addiction to fossil fuels and our willingness to blind ourselves to the effects that such non-sustainable choices have on our only planet - the one we will be leaving to our children - are truly monstrous. <!--break-->The following - from the <a href="http://www.theglobeandmail.com/globe-investor/congress-accuses-bp-of-cutting-corners-to-create-nightmare-well/article1604215/">Toronto Globe and Mail</a> - made what little hair I have left stand up:</p><blockquote><p>Suspending the [BP shareholder] dividend to meet American political demands could set a dangerous international precedent, they said. It would in effect allow other countries to insist that any oil company found responsible for a big spill suspend its dividend until all damages are paid. &ldquo;This is a decision that BP should not take lightly.&nbsp;</p></blockquote><p>Shouldn&#39;t we demand that all oil companies be responsible? &nbsp;Shouldn&#39;t those investing in or insuring oil companies be demanding that all needed safety measures are taken? &nbsp;Ditto for coal and the environmental catastrophes it causes - not to mention the lives they both claim. &nbsp;Yes, such measures will probably increase the costs of fossil fuels, but isn&#39;t it time that the US gets out of the Mideast and declares war on fossil fuels? &nbsp;Talk about creating jobs! &nbsp;Let&#39;s put people to work building mass transit, creating new energy grids carrying solar, wind and probably (I can&#39;t believe I&#39;m saying this) nuclear power. &nbsp;Let&#39;s make sustainable electricity and electric vehicles. &nbsp;And while we&#39;re at it, let&#39;s promote locavore initiatives and learn to eat and live locally. &nbsp;And why are half of our elected officials against such measures? &nbsp;One excellent way to support these initiatives is to educate your neighbors. &nbsp;If you&#39;ve got some ideas or stories to tell along these lines, please add your comments. &nbsp;Maybe we can start a movement for sanity. &nbsp;Or support a group like <a href="http://nextagenda.com/">NextAgenda</a>.</p>
