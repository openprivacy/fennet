<p>Our deepest fear is not that we are inadequate.<br>Our deepest fear is that we are powerful beyond measure.<br>It is our light, not our darkness that most frightens us.<br>We ask ourselves, who am I to be brilliant, gorgeous talented and fabulous?<br>Actually, who are you not to be?<br>You are a child of God.<br>Your playing small doesn't serve the world. There's nothing enlightened<br>about shrinking so that other people won't feel insecure around you.<br>We were born to make manifest the glory of God that is within us.<br>It's not just in some of us, it's in everyone.<br>And as we let our own light shine, we unconsciously give other people permission to do the same.<br>As we are liberated from our own fear, our presence automatically liberates others.<br>&nbsp;</p>
