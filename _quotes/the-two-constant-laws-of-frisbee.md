<p>Keep in mind always the two constant Laws of Frisbee:</p><ol><li>The most powerful force in the world is that of a disc straining to land under a car, just out of reach (this force is technically termed <i>car suck</i>).</li><li>Never precede any maneuver by a comment more predictive than "Watch this!"</li></ol>
