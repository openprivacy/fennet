<p><strong><u>How to fix people</u> (listening for engineers :-)</strong></p><ul><li>People have really good built in diagnostics.</li><li>If you listen well the will tell you what is wrong.</li><li>If you listen really well they will hear what is wrong.</li><li>If you're an expert listener they will hear what is wrong and realize they no longer need fixing.</li></ul>
