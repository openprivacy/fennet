<p>If you have time to chatter,</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Read a book.</p><p>If you have time to read,</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Walk into mountain, desert and ocean.</p><p>If you have time to walk,</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Sing songs and dance.</p><p>If you have time to dance,</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Sit quietly, you Happy Lucky Idiot.</p>
